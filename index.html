Woodpecker is a team of professional in the wood processing
and the creation of wooden furniture of the highest class. We
are a family of artisans and designers who believe that to make
things well, you must put your heart in it. Unique, quality wood
products you will keep forever. We are glad to have you around!



Our tema takes over everything,form an idea and concept
development to realizatoin. We belive in traditoins and
incorporate them within our innovations. All our projects
incorporate a unique artistic image and functional soluctions.
Clients is the soul fo the projects. Our main goal is to illustrate
his/hers values and individuality through design. Our clients are
daring and successful people, businessmen and celebrities,IT
compaines and large banks, media companies and creative
agencies, store and restaurant chains, international developing
compaines, educational centers and public institutions. All our
clients have one prevalling trait-they are bold and modren, they
like to stay ahead of trends, and work with the best.
